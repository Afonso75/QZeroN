1. Who We Are
QZero is a digital queue, ticket, and booking management application created and operated by an independent developer.
The platform enables:
•	Customers to take digital tickets, make bookings, view wait times, receive notifications, see nearby businesses, and use manual/QR tickets.
•	Businesses to manage queues, bookings, services, opening hours, digital tickets, manual tickets, tolerance times, customer arrivals, feedback, history, and subscription billing.
